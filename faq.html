The proper room size is 10 ft (L) x 10 ft (W) x 10 ft (H) for a 1.0 Ton capacity air conditioner. In a 10 feet high room, a split type AC should be installed at 7-8 feet for the best cooling performance. For a window type AC, the reccomended height is 3-4 feet from the floor. If you are fixing the AC very close to the wall or in an airtight room it will trip due to high head pressure, as heat dissipation cannot happen.
In a VarioQool Inverter air conditioner, cool air is delivered by varying the frequency of the compressor, and thus regulating the refrigerant to satisfy room cooling requirements. This helps achieve the desired temperature with minimal fluctuations and no cut-in and cut-off sounds. It eliminates waste with precise control. In this way, it is more energy efficient. While in the case of fixed speed air conditioner the compressor operates at a fixed speed or shuts off when desired temperature is achieved.
"While selecting any split ac for your home it is crucial to understand the tonnage suitable for that room. To determine the required tonnage you need to primarily consider the total are of the room. Also important is the size of windows, location of the walls, exposure to sunlight and air circulation and the other electronic gadgets in the room, It is recommended to request for the technician to visit the room and allow him to do a heat load calculation. A thumb rule of 100 sq. ft = 1.0 ton is usually applied to calculate the tonnage. However the same is just for estimation and not considered to be accurate."

  • Temperature setting - the electricity consumption of air conditioners depends on the thermostat temperature. The lower you keep the air conditioner temperature; the higher will be the electricity consumption.
  • Other electronic appliances in the room - All electrical appliances emit some amount of heat in the room which eventually gets cooled by the air conditioner. Even bulbs and lights emit heat along with the light. Thus if a room has a lot of lights, it will need more air conditioning - hence increasing electricity consumption.
  • Clogged air filters lead to higher consumption of electricity to perform regular operations. Hence cleaning dust filters regularly is highly recommended.

​COOL Mode is a default mode in which the AC runs as per the temperature setting, fan speed and swing setting at which it is set. Keep pressing the MODE button till you see (snowflake icon). Then proceed to adjust the temperature by using the (up & down arrow) and set the temperature. You will also have to adjust the FAN SPEED and SWING for optimum cooling. The ideal temperature for thermal comfort and electricity saving is 25-degree Celsius​.
The multiple AC modes mentioned below help you in experiencing optimum cooling. However, it is important to adjust the Fan speed and swing for a better cooling experience. You can select these modes by pressing the MODE button until the icon of the mode you require is displayed on the remote display.
  • AUTO MODE (icon)- The AC automatically sets the temperature and fan speed depending on the room temperature. Set temperature can’t be adjusted and will not be displayed as well.
  • DRY MODE (icon) - The AC operates at low fan speed to remove excess humidity in the room. This helps in keeping your room cool and dry.
  • HEAT MODE (icon) - The AC performs the heating operation by blasting air to warm your room. You can use adjust the temperature, fan speed and swing as per requirement.
  • TURBO MODE (icon) - Under COOL or HEAT mode, press this button to turn to warm or cool your room quickly. In this mode, the room is cooled effectively as the fan speed operates at the maximum speed.
TIMER allows you to operate the AC depending on your personal requirement for specified time of your choice. After setting the required temperature press the TIMER ON/OFF button once and then adjust the time when you wish the AC to switch OFF using the (up or down button). Press TIMER ON/OFF button again to set the TIMER OFF.
TIMER ON allows you to switch ON the AC after the TIMER OFF operation is done. Select TIMER ON/OFF button once and adjust the time when you wish the AC to switch ON. using the (up or down button). Press TIMER ON/OFF button again to set the TIMER ON. Note that the AC needs to be ON and the CLOCK should be set as per actual time to set both the timers. TIMER ON can be set only after you have set the TIMER OFF. You can cancel the timer funtion by selecting the TIMER ON/OFF button anytime when the function is on.
To set the temperature switch ON the AC and select the Cooling Mode. If you do not see the (snowflake icon) then you need set the MODE to COOLING MODE. Follow the steps provided in the COOLING Mode description above to do so.
