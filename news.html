Tips for using air conditioners in the summer to save electricity bills

July 30, 2021

Show at is the time of the year is dry with the weather getting hotter and hotter. In addition, the impact of COVID-19 services also makes people stay at home to work more and the requirement to use air conditioning also increases. So I made this video to share with you how to use your computer to save electricity.


Use Econo automatic energy saving mode on remote

Normally, air-conditioners have automatic energy-saving running mode. On Daikin FTKM air conditioners, the energy saving feature is called Econo. This function minimizes power consumption. It helps to reduce energy consumption if the refrigerant load is high. Of course, the maximum power reduction while using ECONO mode makes the air conditioner take longer to reach the set temperature. So you should use this mode on a day when it's not too hot or at night.

Another mode to help the machine save more power is the Quiet mode. This mode will reduce the operating capacity of the compressor to optimize power saving and at the same time bring quietness to the outdoor unit. Of course, when such a reduction, the machine needs longer operating time to reach the desired temperature. Speaking of quiet, after a while of using this 1-horse FTKM line, I really like the smoothness of operation of this machine. The machine runs smoothly like no noise, suitable for sleeping or when you need to work to concentrate.


Avoid constantly turning on/off the air conditioner

If you are out for a period of less than an hour, turning off the air conditioner and reopening it many times is not necessarily an optimal saving solution. Restarting the air conditioner causes the engine to restart from the beginning to reach stable performance. With inverter technology, it will maintain optimal operation when the room temperature is stable.


Night set mode

Using the on-off timer feature on the remote also helps you activate Night mode. This mode helps to avoid overheating or being too cold while you sleep. After being activated, after 60 minutes, the set temperature will increase by 0.5°C to help the machine save more electricity.


Use standard smart eye mode

Based on the above principles I shared, Daikin FTKM air conditioners develop sensor-enabled technology (called smart eyes). This is essentially an infrared sensor that can recognize people present in the room. When you go out for about 20 minutes for a period of time, this sensor will help the machine increase the temperature by 2 degrees Celsius. When you return, the machine will automatically return to the previously set temperature. This smart solution will give you the feeling that going into a room is already cool and comfortable and the machine does not have to work hard, saving electricity and making no noise. The fact that the air conditioner is equipped with an infrared sensor is not new. But now I only know that the Daikin FTKM series has optimized this feature to automatically increase and decrease the temperature when the user goes out of the room to save more energy. Just press the Sensor button on the remote together with the green light showing the status right above the On / Off button of the air conditioner, you will know that the smart eye mode is active.


Combined with the use of fans and air conditioners

In an enclosed space with high temperature, you can open the doors and use a fan to release the hot air in the room. Besides, the hot air flow in the room usually exists above the ceiling. Therefore, turning on the air conditioner towards the floor can help you cool down when not near the machine, but it takes time for the room air flow to be dissipated more thoroughly. So, a small trick is to use a ceiling fan to circulate the air in combination with the cold wind from the air conditioner.

Besides, if you both want to save money and want to be cooler, you can use a fan with the air conditioner. If the temperature is stable, the airflow from the fan will help you feel comfortable without having to adjust the temperature to low.

To support better airflow on the ceiling, many manufacturers have made many improvements in the design of the wing to help the air flow to the ceiling more. For example, Daikin FTKM air conditioners have an indoor unit mask design with a curved angle at the air outlet to take advantage of the Coanda airflow science effect. The wind blowing from the air conditioner will go along the ceiling and then spread evenly throughout the room, avoiding the case that the machine always blows cold air into only a certain area in the room, leading to sitting in the same room while others are not. freezing cold while others are still hot. Then the other person feels hot and adjusts the temperature down, causing the machine to run more power.


Use the timer feature on the remote, or smart infrared controls

In addition, you can combine the use of an on-off timer to optimize for different uses. I usually set a timer to turn off the air conditioner at midnight and turn it back on in the morning to save a little more power, because I usually don't wake up suddenly and it's easy to go back to sleep until I have enough sleep. Besides, if your air conditioner has a smartphone-link feature, you can use the smartphone app to monitor the operation of the air conditioner from there. Then we can know if the air conditioner is working or not? From there, it can be turned off to save electricity in case we go out for a long time and forget to turn it off.


Basic air conditioner cleaning

There have been many posts about this content, I repeat it for you to follow. When you need more thorough cleaning, you can rely on specialized cleaning services from reputable and experienced units. Each air conditioner has a large dust filter located inside the cooler. Removing the cap to remove this filter is very easy. So if you have free time on the weekend to clean the house, you can easily climb up the high chair or ladder to take out the dust filters in the air conditioner, about 1-2 times a month is fine, more is fine. .

In addition, placing the heater in the right position will also optimize energy savings for the machine because it will reduce the impact with the sun, causing the cooling efficiency to decrease. Place the heater in a cool place, 15 cm away from the wall so that the heat emitted from the heater is dissipated and should be placed in a place to avoid rain and wind to reduce the possibility of the outdoor unit being corroded. To clean the outdoor unit if possible, you just need to use a low-pressure cleaner to spray to reduce dust. Do not spray with strong pressure because it can bend the aluminum foil of the heater. The released dust will not obstruct the air flow, thus further improving the cooling efficiency. The choice of outdoor unit can also contribute to the overall level of cooling efficiency of the machine. Some manufacturers choose to optimize the fan system to increase heat dissipation for the outdoor unit. Daikin FTKM air conditioners use Microchannel heat exchangers with Daikin's special aluminum alloy to improve the conduction system to be smaller, lighter and increase the heat exchange area. With the rapid heat exchange of the aluminum alloy heat exchanger, it saves a lot of energy.
