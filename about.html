  • About Cosy

    Welcome to CosyCompany, your number one source for all things product, air conditional. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of air conditional, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and the best of quality. Founded in 2020 by Cosy Company, CosyStore has come a long way from its beginnings in a starting location , 8A Ton That Thuyet, My Dinh, Ha Noi. When we first started out, we passion for providing the best equipment for customer drove us to start-up and gave us the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers in over place, 220E Front St. Burlington NC, and we are happy to become one of our customers' trusted places.We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

    Sincerely,

    Tony, CEO

  • Mission

    COSY is committed to bringing the best personal air quality to consumers and businesses around the world through superior technology & engineering, and to elevate daily living into an art form. With the guideline is "Continuous development for customers" and satisfy customers by creating added values ​​such as providing prestigious and professional products and services.

  • Vission

    The number one company in Vietnam in the field of distribution and retail of electrical products, computers, mobile phones and furniture.

  • Strategic Objectives

    • 1. Maximize the investment value of shareholders; maintain profit growth and healthy financial position.
    • 2. Constantly improving working motivation and staff capacity; COSY must always lead the electronics and furniture industry in creating and developing remuneration policies and career advancement opportunities for its staff.
    • 3. Maintain customer satisfaction, loyalty and attachment to COSY; building COSY into one of the leading companies in Vietnam with very prestigious and professional service quality chosen by customers.
    • 4. Developing COSY into one of the leading electrical and furniture systems in Vietnam in terms of: good management, good working environment, customer-focused corporate culture, promoting cooperation and creativity and being very flexible as the business environment changes.

  • Commitment

    • 1. Commitment to partners:

      Becoming a strategic partner at home and abroad on the basis of "Cooperation, sustainable development" for long-term comprehensive cooperation in order to promptly introduce new products and services according to the specific requirements of customers.

    • 2. Commitment to customers:

      - Always make customers satisfied with the products and services provided by COSY. The success of customer satisfaction is a measure of the business's effective reputation.

      - Commodity prices are always reasonable and updated accurately and timely to serve customers maximum.

      - Always listen, analyze and learn from domestic and foreign markets, never be complacent with existing success.

      - Always look at yourself to develop (ethics and professional knowledge). Every employee is a personal brand. Each employee is a COSY goodwill ambassador to the outside world.
